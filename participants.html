Participants

There are four participating schools in this project. Gottlieb-Daimler-Schule 1 from Germany, Strömbackaskolan from Sweden, Istituto d'istuzione superiore POLO 3 from Italy and Lykeio Palouriotissas from Cyprus. A brief introduction of the different schools follows below.

Gottlieb-Daimler-Schule 1

Die Gottlieb-Daimler-Schule 1 ist eine berufliche Schule, die berufliche Aus- und Weiterbildungen mit den Schwerpunkten KFZ, Metall und Farbe anbietet.
Zu ihr gehört ein technisches Gymnasium, das in drei Jahren zum Abitur führt und die Profilfächer "Technik und Management", "Informationstechnik", "Gestaltungs- und Medientechnik" sowie "Mechatronik" anbietet.
An der Schule unterrichten 115 Lehrer 1950 Schüler, von denen viele in einer dualen Ausbildung sind.
Unsere Schule liegt in einem Wirtschaftsraum mit international ausgerichteten Unternehmen. Um den Anforderungen dieser Unternehmen gerecht zu werden, gilt es unser internationales Profil zu schärfen.
Dazu soll die interkulturelle Kompetenz und persönliche Entwicklung der Schüler gestärkt werden. Ihre Fremdsprachenkenntnisse sollen vertieft und die Motivation am Fremdsprachenlernen gestärkt werden.
Begleitet wird das Projekt von einem Team aus zwei Lehrkräften – Kerstin Oswald und Andreas Richter – die schon mehrere transnationale Partnerschaftsprojekte durchgeführt haben.
Der Projektkoordinator – Andreas Richter – hat als internationaler Koordinator eines Comenius Projektes und einer transnationalen Partnerschaft nach Leitaktion 2 Erfahrungen mit europäischen Schulprojekten gesammelt.
Frau Oswald hat durch ihren Unterricht im Fach Gesellschafts- und Gemeinschaftskunde Kenntnisse zum Klimawandel.
Herr Richter ist Diplom Physiker und hat profunde Naturwissenschaftliche Kenntnisse zum Klimawandel.

Strömbackaskolan

1) General information
Strömbackaskolan is a municipal upper secondary school in Piteå municipality with 1450 pupils and 200 staff. Strömbackaskolan offers 14 national programs, the individual program and a specially designed program specializing in dance. About half of the students choose to apply to vocational programs, and half of them head for university studies. The school has a close cooperation with local companies and we focus on entrepreneurship and young enterprise. The school also focus on Education for Sustainable Development (ESD) and we are trying to be in the frontline when it comes to technic and automation. The school strives to develop international cooperation such as experience exchanges for teachers and students. Piteå Municipality has a focus of developing a “Sustainable Municipality” and to carry out this the schools has a central role as educating the new generations of citizens. Therefore, it is crucial for all schools to focus on Education for sustainable development and one of the ways of doing this is to participate in different international projects. The school has just ended one ERASMUS+ project called "European student climate report" and this is a follow up on that project. As Sustainability has been targeting, the school has developed methods for working in that field. One of them is the energy and environmental role-playing game, where students in grade one have been acting countries and occupy positions of the various countries to debate the issues. Strömbacka school has also got the award of "School for sustainable development" (Skola för hållbar utveckling)", an award that the Swedish National Agency for Education is responsible for.

2) motivation
Around us, locally, nationally and globally we see a growing concern for the changes in environment regarding weather patterns and the consequences thereof. Being involved in a project like this will further deepen the knowledge and awareness among students and teachers so we can better understand the reasons for what is happening. We want to learn more about the political actions, which have been taken in Europe and what impact they have had for the environment. We also want to investigate what can be done in the future by political leaders and decision makers in Europe to help improve the current environmental status.
